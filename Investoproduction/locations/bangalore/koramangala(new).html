Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have private cabins for large teams ?

No, currently we don't have any cabins or private offices at our studios

Do you have custom plans for larger teams ?

Yes, we devise custom workplace solutions depending on your team size and requirements. Please fill up a form with your details and we will get back to you with a quote ASAP.

What is your cancellation policy?

Coworkers can cancel plans anytime before or on the last day of the month.

What is the process for payments?

Unfortunately, we do not accept credit cards or online payments. However, we do accept NEFT bank transfers or cheques.Contact Us for info.

What are your tax charges?

Currently under GST, we charge 18% tax. All price plans are exclusive of taxes.

Can I walk-in to visit the space?

Yes, you may show up to any Investopad location for a walk through of the space. However, we prefer if you book a tour here, so that we can attend to you immediately.

Are there charges for printing & scanning?

No. Coworker may print as they need. However, we ask that you do not abuse this privilege.

Can I use the other Investopad locations?

Yes. Members may sit at any Investopad location. However, Investopad management must be notified first so we can let the other spaces know they are expecting you. .
